About Nivedini

Nivedini is a lightweight digital assistant built to empower ASHA workers with efficient tools for patient management, report documentation, and incentive tracking, all in one place.

Our Mission

India's grassroots healthcare system relies heavily on ASHA workers. Yet, most still depend on paper records and face tech barriers. This app aims to bridge that gap with easy-to-use features for voice and image input, auto-summarization, and seamless record management, available even for low-digital-literacy users.
